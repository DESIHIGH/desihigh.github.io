About DESI

DESI (Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument) is a revolutionary astronomical survey that aims to map the large-scale structure of the universe and understand the nature of dark energy. By measuring the spectra of millions of galaxies and quasars, DESI will provide unprecedented insights into the expansion history of the universe and the distribution of dark matter.

About DESI High

DESI High is a free online platform designed to help high school students learn about astronomy and data science through hands-on experience with real astronomical data.

Our goal is to make astronomy accessible and engaging for students, providing them with the tools and resources they need to explore the universe.
